Hidden Gems

Escape to a tropical paradise unlike any other - welcome to Marinduque! This heart-shaped island province boasts breathtaking natural wonders, a rich cultural heritage, and a unique location, making it the perfect destination for every traveler.

Bagumbayang Cave

Go spelunking through the magnificent Bagumbayang Cave in Marinduque! With its breathtaking rock formations and waist-deep underground river basin, this spot is perfect for adventurers of all levels.

Palad Sandbar

Feel the thrill of paddleboarding in crystal-clear, waist-deep waters. With the beach just a few hundred meters away, this strip of white sand is suitable for water sports enthusiasts.

Amoingon Beach

See the vibrant underwater world at Amoingon Beach in Marinduque! Known as the snorkeling capital of the island, its clear waters are teeming with colorful marine life. Not a fan of snorkeling? No worries, enjoy the fresh air and sunshine above the water.

Poctoy White Beach

Get a taste of paradise at Poctoy Beach in Torrijos, Marinduque! This beautiful expanse of white sand, surrounded by crystal clear waters, is perfect for swimming, kayaking, and relaxing in beachfront huts and cottages. Discover the underwater beauty of the Poctoy Underwater Museum.

Maniwaya Island

Make Maniwaya Island your next bucket list destination! With its pristine white-sand beaches, budget-friendly Santa Cruz beach resorts, and Bali-inspired glamping cottages, it rivals the best in the Philippines. Don't miss the Palad Sandbar off the northern coast, best enjoyed in the morning during low tide.

Malbog Sulfur Hotspring

Surrounded by lush greenery, Malbog Sulfur Hotspring offers a therapeutic escape with warm, bluish-white sulfuric water believed to have healing benefits. With two swimming pools and medicinal properties that can cure skin diseases, it's perfect for a rejuvenating dip.
